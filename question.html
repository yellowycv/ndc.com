It is a Non-Profit Organization representing all ethnic groups of Nasarawa people to bring development and empowerment at the community and society level. What is the Restoration Plan all about?

It is an effort to reform the world systems in line with the Natural law. To shift from a debt-based system to an asset/knowledge-based transitional economic system. It is a plan to restore the earth to her state of health before the darkness took over control.

What is the Global Repository?

The Global Repository was established by the Trustee as a new designation for the financial aspect of the Quantum Mapping System of the World Trust. It is a replacement for all Central Banks and the Federal Reserve Bank, which is the custodian of all World currencies and the only tier 1 bank. Now all countries have a Repository connected with the assets of each country.

Who can participate?

Everybody of goodwill who has ideas, expertise, businesses, projects that can benefit the people. Entrepreneurs (self-employed and business owners) are essential in this. But everyone who has something to give to improve Nigeria, no matter how small. We need people from all sectors of the economy in all the value chains, from harnessing the natural resources, processing, manufacturing, logistics to retailing, who will follow our ethical guidelines to take charge and gradually replace the Monopolies.

How can one participate?

You need to have a project. It could be a new or existing business, idea, innovation, talent, or anything that will uplift humanity and the environment. Then you join the Council by volunteering.

Are the funds in the form of grants? Grants are controversial, what is the guarantee?

Yes, the funds come in the form of grants because a grant is the only available word used to describe funds that are not a loan. Grants are usually given by individuals or organizations that have made a lot of money and want to give back as a humanitarian gesture. It is different here. Here we have an institution that doesn't make money but is holding all the natural resources or assets of the earth and converts them to money. So we are talking about an entitlement of every human being. It is not a grant in the normal sense. We have the opportunity to access the Repository for Nigeria in the Global Repository of World Trust.

What is CARE?

CARE is an acronym for Center for Amity and Restoration of Earth. It is a replacement for the United Nations (UN) and serves as the engine or channel to achieve the restoration of planet Earth.
